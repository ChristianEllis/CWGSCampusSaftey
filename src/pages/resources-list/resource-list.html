Know your rights.

If you or a friend has been a victim of sexual assault, it is important to understand the following:
  • You may seek medical care without reporting the crime to the police.
  • You have a right to confidentiality.
  • It is your right to decide what to do.
  • It is not your fault.
  • Your feelings are valid & important.
  • You are entitled to emotional support.
  • Please seek medical attention within 72 hours to ensure your safe.
